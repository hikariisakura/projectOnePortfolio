All about Maika

Maika was born on the 12th of September in 2002 in Guangzhou, China. When she was 7, she moved to California, United States of America. Her whole surroundings changed and as she struggled through language barrier, she adapted to her new life, vastly different from China.

Maika found her passion for art and design in anime. During her school years, she would often draw anime characters or read mangas borrowed from the local library. As she grew older, she discovered many interests. Maika's hobbies contains a mix of watching videos, gaming, reading, art, and listening to music. All these hobbies makes her who she is. In high school, Maika's friend forced a music genre known as K-pop to her. She slowly came to love the genre and became a collector of K-pop. Her favorite groups contains Seventeen, The Boyz, Stray Kids, and Astro. Maika became a collector of K-pop merchandise especially the idol Minyu from Seventeen. As of today she has stopped collecting and moved but continues to listen to K-pop. Maika currently loves to listen to J-pop, K-pop, and C-pop whenever she can.

Romance has always been a favorite genre for Maika. She would often read right before bed each day. With the rising popularity of Boys Love (yaoi), she fell in love with it. Boys Love is a controversial topic and was often seen negatively. But as the world began to slowly accept LGBTQ, she was delighted more and more novels, and manga of such genre are being translated to English for her to read. Maika's biggest obsession currently is with the rise of Chinese Boys Love (denmei).

Maika's design style word is cute. She is a big fan of cute things and her favorite palette to use is pastel and muted colors, especially pinks and blues. She has hopes of landing in a career where she can be surrounded by art and design.
